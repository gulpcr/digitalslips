## ðŸŽ¯ Business Problem Being Solved

### **Current Challenges:**

#### 1. **Manual Receipt Generation**
- **Problem**: Tellers manually print receipts for every transaction
- **Impact**: 
  - Time-consuming (3-5 minutes per transaction)
  - High paper costs (PKR 2M+ annually)
  - Environmental concerns
  - Poor customer experience during peak hours

#### 2. **Receipt Verification Issues**
- **Problem**: Customers lose paper receipts, disputes arise
- **Impact**:
  - Customer complaints increase
  - Branch staff spend hours verifying old transactions
  - Fraud risks with forged receipts
  - Regulatory compliance challenges

#### 3. **Limited Customer Communication**
- **Problem**: No instant transaction notifications
- **Impact**:
  - Customers don't know transaction status immediately
  - Missed fraud detection opportunities
  - Poor customer satisfaction scores

#### 4. **Audit Trail Gaps**
- **Problem**: Paper receipts difficult to track and audit
- **Impact**:
  - Compliance issues with State Bank of Pakistan (SBP)
  - Difficult to investigate disputes
  - No centralized transaction records
